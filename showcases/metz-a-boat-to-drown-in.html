A Boat to Drown In by METZ

Single Showcase

METZ are a Canadian punk band that have been together for over a decade, in that time they have released three studio albums along with a compilation record. With the release of this single they announced their fourth album, Atlas Vending, which will be dropping in October. I saw METZ live when they were the support for IDLES at the Barrowlands in December, and their energetic noise rock made me an instant fan.

This single, A Boat to Drown In will be the closer on this upcoming album, and chimes in at over 7 minutes in length making it their longest released song yet. The group describes the song as being about leaving a bad situation behind and about overcoming obstacles that once held you back. The guitar riff that repeats throughout the song is catchy and so good. Also shoutout to the basically non-stop drum beat, it makes the track feel like it's whirring around. This single makes me very excited for the album.

July 2020
