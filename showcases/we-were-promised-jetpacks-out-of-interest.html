out of interest by We Were Promised Jetpacks

EP Showcase

out of interest is the latest EP from Scottish indie rockers We Were Promised Jetpacks. The new release consists of three previously unreleased songs, and two demos of songs from their 2018 album The More I Sleep the Less I Dream. The KEXP performance that followed the release of that album is what brought me to the band, and it continues to be one of my favourite albums in my library. Also, they produced the album at the home studio of Aaron Dessner (of The National) - which is pretty cool.

Regarding the demos, not wanted and impossible, they're both pretty close to the studio takes. The main difference comes with the lyrics, and impossible seems to have went through a complete lyrical overhaul between this demo version and the finished thing.

The previously unreleased songs all sound like they could have been placed on The More I Sleep the Less I Dream without sticking out, so it's nice that we're able to hear them. The opener, when getting list, has a long instrumental ending with some pretty cool spiralling guitars. The next song same mistakes is a probably my favourite on the EP, and has lyrics that I can imagine a crowd shouting at a gig - It's all in my head, Making the same mistakes I did.

Now I guess it's time to wait until their fifth album is ready, but until then I'll happily keep listening to this EP and their other previous work.

July 2020
