Human by Grace Bland

Single Showcase

Grace Bland is an indie pop singer-songwriter from the UK. Influenced by artists such as Lana Del Ray and Maisie Peters, she blends acoustic pop, folk and rock to create something that is new and exciting. Grace has competed in Open Mic UK and in 2019 reached the grand final, being described as a star in the making by the judges. Her Spotify debut, Pity Parties, has been played on numerous radio stations, most notably being featured on BBC Introducing Radio Solent in October 2019.

Her new single, Human, which will be released July 27th, is a piano ballad where Grace explores how she thinks of herself. Lyrics like And find it hard to like myself some days and I over think \ And overthink \ And overthink really build a connection between her and the listener as it is something that everyone goes though. Like the name suggests, it's a Human thing to try and make ourselves something that we aren't - and the chorus on this track encapsulates that idea perfectly. The songs also ends really strongly, by repeating the chorus but with a contrasting final line, But I'm happy with the human in me.

Grace is currently running a giveaway where you can win an embroidered Human t-shirt, which you can enter here. Make sure you give the track a listen when it drops later this week, you won't be disappointed.
