Night Skin, Pt. 1 by Mauvey

EP Showcase

Mauvey is a Ghanaian born and UK raised artist and songwriter that beautifully blends genres like hip hop, R&B and soul into his music to create a very unique sound. I first heard Mauvey when he played in a church in St Andrews earlier this year and it was a truly incredible experience. Since then he has released many more singles, each bringing something new and exciting to the table.

The release prior to this one (The Bull and The Matador) instantly caught the attention of BBC Radio Solent, crowning it the Record of the Week, and deservedly so. That release brought out his most streamed song to date, The Matador, alongside a B-side that in my opinion is just as good. Less than a month later, Mauvey is back with the first part of an EP called Night Skin containing two more songs to add to his quickly expanding catalogue.

With this release, Mauvey delivers two more fantastic tracks, once again blending genres together to make something different. It's hard to pick a favourite here, but I think Odyssey just edges it for me. A short track with plenty of action. Goldie is right up there in terms of my favourite Mauvey songs too, but then again, so is every song he's released so far.

Alongside this release he has also released a merch line, which you can check out here. All the profit from this line is going towards a grant which he is putting together to help black creative artists. You can also donate directly to the cause by using the fundraiser linked on his Spotify page (or here), if that's something you would rather do. If you're not in a position to donate or buy merch, no worries, it's a tough time for everyone at the moment, but make sure to stream the EP - it's really good!

June 2020
